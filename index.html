Our Legacy of Learning

Located in Mohanpur Kamalpur, Siraha, our institution has been a beacon of knowledge for generations, offering a complete educational path from Class 1 to Bachelor's level.

Expert & Dedicated Faculty

Our team of highly qualified and experienced teachers is the backbone of our institution, committed to nurturing each student's unique potential.

Holistic Development

We emphasize a balanced approach to education, focusing on sports, arts, and community service for the all-round development of our students.

Our Mission and Vision

At Shree Janata Madhyamik Vidyalaya, we believe in providing accessible, high-quality education to every student. Our mission is to create a nurturing and intellectually stimulating environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and a lifelong love for learning. We aim to equip our students with the skills and values necessary to succeed in a rapidly changing world and become responsible global citizens.

Since our inception, we have strived for excellence in academics, while fostering a strong sense of community and moral values. Join us as we continue our journey of educational leadership.

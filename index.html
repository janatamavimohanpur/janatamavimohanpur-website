Welcome to Our Legacy of Learning

Shree Janata Madhyamik Vidyalaya, located in the heart of Mohanpur Kamalpur, Siraha, has been a beacon of knowledge for generations. Established with the vision to make quality education accessible to all, we have grown from a humble beginning into a full-fledged institution offering education up to the Bachelor's level. Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to succeed in an ever-changing world.

Our sprawling campus provides a serene and conducive environment for learning. We believe in a blend of traditional values and modern teaching methodologies to create a learning experience that is both effective and engaging. We invite you to be a part of our journey.

Our Academic Wings

A structured path for every stage of learning.

Primary Level (Class 1-8)

Building strong foundational skills in a fun, interactive, and supportive environment. We focus on literacy, numeracy, and curiosity.

Secondary Level (Class 9-12)

Preparing students for national-level examinations (SEE & +2) with a rigorous curriculum and expert faculty in Science, Management, and Arts.

Bachelor's Level

Offering undergraduate degrees that equip students with the professional knowledge and skills required for successful careers in their chosen fields.
